What we do

End-to-end interoperability services

From architecture design to implementation and training — we cover the full lifecycle of healthcare integration projects.

FHIR Consulting

Expert guidance on implementing FHIR R4/R5 architectures and APIs. We help you leverage the full power of the standard for modern health data exchange.

openEHR Integration

Specialized consulting in openEHR implementations and the co-existence of openEHR and FHIR through our FHIR Connect specification.

Enterprise Architecture

Design of scalable, interoperable healthcare IT architectures for hospitals, clinics, and national health programs — built for the long term.

System Integration

Complex integrations with Epic, Cerner Millennium, ChipSoft HiX, and other major EMR/EHR platforms using IHE profiles and messaging standards.

IHE Compliance

Implementation of IHE profiles including XDS, MPI, XDW, ATNA, QEDm, and SMART on FHIR to ensure standards-based, auditable interoperability.

Training & Education

Comprehensive training on healthcare interoperability standards, FHIR implementation, and best practices — tailored for health IT teams at all levels.

Our Product

openFHIR: Bridging openEHR and FHIR

As co-creators of the FHIR Connect specification and founders of openFHIR, we built an engine for seamless bidirectional interoperability between openEHR and HL7 FHIR — giving you the clinical richness of openEHR with the connectivity of FHIR.

  • Bidirectional mapping between FHIR Resources and openEHR Compositions
  • RESTful API and message queue support for flexible integration
  • Docker-ready deployment for rapid rollout
  • Atlas interface for mapping management and data analysis
  • Built on the open-source FHIR Connect specification
